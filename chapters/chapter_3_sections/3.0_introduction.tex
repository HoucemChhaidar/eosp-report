\section*{Introduction}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Introduction}

After validating the architecture and specifying the requirements, the first \emph{release} of Credix unfolds in two four-week sprints: Sprint 1 focuses on functional foundations and security (JWT authentication, role-based user management with three-tier hierarchy, and automatic wallet creation), while Sprint 2 concentrates on transaction management (wallet balance viewing, transaction history, store listings, time-bound barcode generation, and real-time transaction confirmation).
